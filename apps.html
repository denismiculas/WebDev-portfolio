Facebook

Facebook is a Social media app where you can post and share posts/pictures/videos. You can go live and communicate thought the separate app, messenger created by the same company.

Warnings/Cautions

Those are 2 big cautions everyone should watch out for:

  • The big number of fake accounts are a big issue on facebook. As there is no proper ways to verify when creating an account, people make fake accounts for different reasons. Such as: paedophilic reason through location and posts; scamming/hacking reasons. Example recently people have been sent a link saying, “is that you in the video?” and if you click on it, hackers find your details as in your password and email.
  • As cyber bullying is a big issue nowadays. You must be careful of what you post as once is on the internet it will never be gone. You must ensure that it won’t insult anyone. Also, that your posts are appropriate for all age groups.

Instagram

Instagram is a social media app that is mainly based around pictures and posts. It is an app where you can communicate with others and share your visual work to the world.

Warnings/Cautions

Those are some basic cautions you should know about:

  • Don’t share your password with anyone.
  • Even if you keep your account limited or private, it can still reach a wider audience
  • Think about what you’re posting. Can someone find you through the picture?
  • Instagram isn’t reality as people only post the best parts of their life
